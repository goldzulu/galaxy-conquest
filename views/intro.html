Welcome to Galaxy Conquest, a realtime multiplayer game where a maximum of six people can play at the same time. It is also an OPTIONAL play to earn game sitting on currently on the Cronos Testnet blockchain. You can simply join or create a new game. Currently there is no lobby so you have to do the co-ordination manually by sharing the room code with your fellow players!

  • Start new invasion
  • Join an invasion


Wager: While optional, you can reap the benefit more by connecting with your wallet. Wager is currently set at 2.5 CRO and if you win, you will get back a total of 4 CRO. If you lose, you will lose 2.5 CRO. It will go into the treasury pool where on ocassion a double rewards or a jackpot will be given out to players!

Remember to connect your wallet first (sometimes you have to logout and login again) and hit the wager button. A message will prompt you when all is good and you can proceed!

Playing: Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move your white craft left or right to escape the bullets from the spaceship. Your goal is to make it to the other side, alive!
